I've been keeping notes for years, and I still think it's one of the highest-leverage
habits I have. Here are a few reasons why.

## Writing is thinking

You don't really understand an idea until you try to explain it. The act of
writing forces the fuzzy parts into focus.

## Small notes compound

A single note is nearly worthless. A thousand notes, lightly connected, become a
map of how your thinking has changed over time.

## You are the audience

Write for the version of you who reads this next month and has completely
forgotten the context. Be kind to that person. Leave breadcrumbs.
